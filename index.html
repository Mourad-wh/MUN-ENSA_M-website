About Our Event

The Cyberdiplomacy and Engineering Event, organized by the MUN ENSA Marrakech Club in partnership with the Self Dev Club under the auspices of Cadi Ayyad University and the National School of Applied Sciences of Marrakech. is a three days initiative inspired by the royal vision of His Majesty King Mohammed VI expressed in his speech on the occasion of the 19th anniversary of the Throne Day.
This event brings together young minds from all fields to explore diplomacy, geopolitics, and technology through roundtable discussions, Model United Nations (MUN) sessions simulating global problem solving, and a Hackathon to turn ideas into practical solutions.
Don't miss out on this incredible opportunity to be part of the experience! Join us from April 18th to 20th 2025, to learn, collaborate, and make an impact. Get your badge, Be there, and make the most of it!

HACKATHON

The ENSA MUN Hackathon 2025, taking place on April 19th and 20th 2025 , is an innovative competition that brings together talented minds to develop sustainable energy solutions.
This year’s theme, "Sahara Energy: Transforming a Desert into a Global Energy Source". challenges participants to explore the immense potential of the Sahara Desert as a renewable energy hub.
Through collaboration, creativity, and expert mentorship, teams will work on cutting-edge ideas that address energy sustainability, environmental impact, and technological advancements.
With a competitive atmosphere and industry-driven insights, this hackathon is the perfect opportunity for participants to shape the future of global energy. Join us in this transformative journey and be part of the change!
